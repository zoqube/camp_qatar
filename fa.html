SECONDMENT
Teach For Qatar can assist Qatari Nationals that are currently employed to be seconded to Teach For Qatar as a Fellow. Your employer may have a number of requirements that you need to meet before this can be approved. As such,Teach For Qatar is unable to guarantee that your employer will allow you to be seconded. If you want further information about this process, please contact a member of the recruitment team.

QUESTIONS
This section is an integral part of the application process, as it is a valuable opportunity for you to highlight the qualities that will determine your success as a Teach For Qatar Fellow. Therefore, please answer all questions in full (within the word limit) and insure that you review them for spelling and grammar, before you submit your application. For further details please refer to the 'Hints and Tips' on the website


A significant role is one which requires a notable leadership level, involves significant responsibility and/or is a very selective post. You can provide an example from employment, university, a volunteering experience or any other extracurricular activity. It is recommended that you tell us about one specific project that you completed as part of this role. Tell us what this role involved; what you were responsible for and what did you achieve.

An example of a challenge could be taken from university, employment, a volunteering experience or any other extracurricular activity. Some examples of challenges are difficult classes, large-scale projects and learning new concepts. It is recommended that you focus only one challenge and explain what the challenge was, the different methods/techniques you used to overcome this challenge and the results you achieved.
